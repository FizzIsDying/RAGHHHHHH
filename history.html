Services

2005

Lush Wave: A Tranquil Retreat Opens its Doors

Lush Wave, a serene and relaxing retreat, was established in 2005, offering guests a tranquil escape from their busy lives.

2008

Expansion and Renovation for Enhanced Comfort

In 2008, Lush Wave underwent an extensive expansion and renovation project to enhance the comfort and amenities offered to guests. The updates included luxurious spa facilities, spacious swimming pools, and beautifully landscaped gardens.

2012

Introduction of Holistic Wellness Programs

Lush Wave introduced holistic wellness programs in 2012, focusing on providing guests with rejuvenating experiences for their mind, body, and soul. The programs included yoga and meditation classes, wellness workshops, and personalized spa treatments.

2015

Award-Winning Spa Recognized for Excellence

Lush Wave's spa was honored with the prestigious "Best Spa in the Region" award in 2015, recognizing the exceptional service, serene ambiance, and skilled therapists who provided guests with unforgettable relaxation experiences.

2018

Sustainability Initiatives and Eco-Friendly Practices

In 2018, Lush Wave implemented sustainable practices to minimize its environmental impact. The hotel introduced energy-efficient systems, waste reduction programs, and collaborated with local communities to support eco-friendly initiatives, ensuring a harmonious relationship with nature.

2020

Virtual Retreats and Online Wellness Programs

Responding to the changing times, Lush Wave introduced virtual retreats and online wellness programs in 2020. Guests could now experience the hotel's tranquil atmosphere and participate in guided meditation sessions, yoga classes, and wellness workshops from the comfort of their own homes.

2022

Expanding Horizons: Opening of New Locations

Building on its success, Lush Wave expanded its presence in 2022, opening new locations in picturesque destinations known for their natural beauty. These new retreats offered guests the same exceptional level of relaxation and rejuvenation, combined with breathtaking surroundings.
